START FUNDRAISER DONATE
Crowdfunding is the process of raising funds online through small donations from a large number of people on the internet. It uses the power of the masses to fund the smallest to the largest initiatives, making sure that lack of funds don't get in the way of good intentions.. In other words, imagine you want to build a house for a stray cat and you need Rs.10,000 to do so. Instead of putting in the entire cost yourself, or asking one or two people to contribute, you ask a 100 people to put in Rs.100 each. This way more people can get involved in your cause and don't feel the burden of donating a large amount. You accomplish your goal with ease!
